Welcome to Fishingland Sarnes

Fishingland Sarnes enjoys an idyllic location on the banks of Sarnesfjorden, 10 km from Honningsvåg and 35 km from the legendary North Cape. Our guests have access to their own floating piers for their boats, as well as private parking for their cars.

The accommodation consists of highly modern self-catering apartments/houses. They all have their own fully equipped kitchen with everything you’ll need for your stay. The living room has a TV and Wi-Fi Internet. After a long day at sea, you can enjoy fantastic views of the mountains or just relax in the hot-tub or sauna.
